# Ricing

Want to customize your Linux setup? Here is some info!

Ricing comes from the car terminology "ricing" or "RICEing". In the car world, RICE stands for Race Inspired Cosmetic Enhancement.

## Compositors
A Linux Compositor is the system component that draws and manages everything you see on screen under a *composited* window system. A full Desktop Evironment comes with it's own Compositor, so this is for advanced users that want to customize their system down to their core. Using a custom compositor doesn't always have the "default" feel that normal Desktops have.
